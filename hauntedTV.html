Haunted Tv

Venturing into the realm of classic horror cinema, I embarked on a project that expands on the idea of the "mirror scare" trope that is used in horror films where a ghost suddenly appears behind the person. The core concept revolves around transforming a passive viewing experience into an interactive engagement, effectively blurring the line between fiction and reality.

A camera is hidden inside an old b&w TV which showcases a realtime feed on the TV like a mirror. The camera feed is run through OpenCV, which runs face and eye detection algorithms to determine when a person is looking at the screen. Based on a random timer script, when a viewer has looked long enough, a ghostly entity is displayed, lurking behind the viewer. When the user looks over their shoulder, the ghost quickly disappears.
