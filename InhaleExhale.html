Inhale, exhale

Inhale, Exhale is an interactive installation that merges art, technology, and environmental consciousness. This dynamic installation, comprised of folded wool fabric shaped based on the Ron Resch tessellation, serves as a visual representation of the real-time air quality of the city in which it resides. As air quality improves, the fabric's origami pattern undulates gently, capturing the harmonious rhythm of cleaner air. When the air quality gets worse it triggers more pronounced and energetic movements, mimicking how our lungs act under air pollution.
