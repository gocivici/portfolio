Star-Track

Star-Track is an educational star-tracking system that can point and track any object in the night sky using its celestial coordinates.

Many computerized telescopes have a type of telescope mount and related software that can automatically point a telescope to astronomical objects that the user selects. Called GoTo mounts. Like a standard equatorial mount, equatorial GoTo mounts can track the night sky by driving the right-ascension axis. However, these mounts are expansive and are useless without a telescope. Star-track fills this gap by providing a cheap and easy-to-use educational tracking device without the need of a telescope.
